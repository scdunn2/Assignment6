Yukon Gold Potato

Yukon Gold is a large cultivar of potato most distinctly characterized by its thin, smooth, eye-free skin and yellow-tinged flesh.

Ratte Potato

The Ratte potato is a small potato with a unique nutty flavor and smooth, buttery texture. The nutty flavour is said to have come from the types of soils the variety is grown in by the French farmers.

Laura Potato

Laura is a mid-season-ripening, firm-fleshed, edible potato. The Laura potato is red-skinned with a rich potato-yellow flesh color and an oval shape. It was first recognised in 1998 in Austria.

Corne de Gatte is a striking elongated, pink-shaded small potato which is highly valued in culinary circles. It is a waxy potato with a nut-like flavour. Due to its special shape the Corne de Gatte is only suitable to be cooked and served without peeling.

Kerrs Pink Potato

Kerr's Pink is a potato cultivar in wide production in Ireland and the United Kingdom and many other countries. Although often quoted as an "Irish potato", the cultivar was actually created by J. Henry of Cornhill, Scotland, in 1907.

So why potatoes?

To my dear potatoes, Putting my love and admiration for you into words is no simple matter. I'd like to start off by noting that you're the most delicious vegetable on the planet. Any dish you're in is always made better by your presence. And the amount of ways in which I can enjoy you are limitless. From roasted potatoes and mashed potatoes to french fries and potato chips, I consume you in a ton of tasty variations all the time. You make my vegan burgers complete and give me something to look forward to on Thanksgiving. When I need a little snack during the day, I reach for a bag of you in chip form.

I don't think there's been a single day in my life that I haven't eaten you in some various fashion, so thank you for constantly feeding me. Not only do I love you for continually keeping me full, but also for what you represent. You remind me that I can be anything. Though you are harvested as a simple tuberous crop with a rough and dirty exterior, you transform into something so much more than that. Others may underrate your ability to be part of a gourmet dish, but you definitely prove them wrong on a regular basis.
